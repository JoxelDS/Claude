# DS Marketing - Custom AI Content Prompts
## Generate Premium Content in Your Brand Voice

---

## YOUR BRAND VOICE PARAMETERS

**Before using ANY prompt, add this context:**

```
Context: You're writing for DS Marketing, a premium full-service marketing agency specializing in social media management, paid advertising, content creation, and branding/design.

Brand voice:
- Confident but not arrogant
- Direct and no-BS
- Premium and sophisticated
- Data-driven and strategic
- Speaks to other professionals and business owners

Avoid:
- Hype and fluff
- Excessive exclamation marks
- Overused marketing buzzwords ("crushing it", "game-changer")
- Salesy language
- Unprofessional slang

Write with authority. Be the expert they trust, not the influencer they follow.
```

## PROMPT 2: Create Premium Carousel Script

```
Write a 10-slide carousel script for DS Marketing about [YOUR SPECIFIC TOPIC].

Brand context: Premium marketing agency with bold black-and-white aesthetic. We work with established businesses, not startups. Our clients value ROI over vanity metrics.

Carousel structure:
Slide 1: Bold hook (one sentence, all caps style, provocative)
Slides 2-8: Main content points (15-20 words per slide, direct and valuable)
Slide 9: Summary of key takeaways
Slide 10: CTA (follow for more strategic marketing insights)

Tone requirements:
- Confident and authoritative
- No fluff - pure value
- Use specific examples or data where possible
- Speak to decision-makers, not entry-level
- Slightly provocative but professional

Make slide 1 impossible to scroll past. Make slides 2-8 worth saving.
```

**Example usage:**
```
[Topic: "Why Your Paid Ads Are Failing"]

Expected output:
Slide 1: "YOU'RE NOT BAD AT ADS. YOUR STRATEGY IS."
Slide 2: "Most businesses throw money at Facebook hoping something sticks. That's not a strategy."
[etc.]
```

## PROMPT 3: Premium Instagram Captions

```

Each caption should include:
1. Opening hook (bold, stops the scroll)
2. Context or credibility statement (2-3 sentences)
3. Value preview (what they'll learn)
```

## BRAND VOICE EXAMPLES

### WRONG (Too Casual/Hype):
"OMG guys!! You NEED to see these INSANE tips that will absolutely BLOW YOUR MIND! Marketing has never been this easy!!!"

### RIGHT (DS Marketing Voice):
"Most agencies overcomplicate marketing. Here's what actually works."

---

### WRONG (Too Corporate/Boring):
"In order to optimize your social media presence, it is imperative that you leverage best practices and implement a holistic strategy."

### RIGHT (DS Marketing Voice):
"Your social media doesn't need more tactics. It needs a strategy. Here's the difference."

---

### WRONG (Too Salesy):
"Don't miss out on this amazing opportunity! Click the link in bio NOW to transform your business TODAY! Limited spots available!!!"

### RIGHT (DS Marketing Voice):
"We work with 12 clients at a time. No more, no less. That's how we deliver results. Currently accepting applications."
