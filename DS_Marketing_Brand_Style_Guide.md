# DS Marketing - Brand Style Guide
## Your Signature Black & White Aesthetic

---

## BRAND IDENTITY

**Brand Name:** DS Marketing
**Positioning:** Premium, full-service marketing agency
**Aesthetic:** Minimalist luxury, editorial sophistication
**Color Philosophy:** Pure monochrome for maximum impact

---

## COLOR SYSTEM

### Primary Colors (ONLY THESE)

**Black:**
- Hex: #000000
- RGB: 0, 0, 0
- Use: Backgrounds, bold text, logo

**White:**
- Hex: #FFFFFF
- RGB: 255, 255, 255
- Use: Text on black, clean backgrounds

**Gray Accents (OPTIONAL - Use Sparingly):**
- Dark Gray: #1A1A1A (for subtle depth)
- Medium Gray: #808080 (for secondary text)
- Light Gray: #F5F5F5 (for subtle backgrounds)

### Color Usage Rules:
- **High contrast always** (pure black on pure white or vice versa)
- **90% of designs should be black + white only**
- **Gray only for subtle texture or secondary elements**
- **Never use colors** (no blues, reds, greens, etc.)
- **Never use gradients**
- **Never use low contrast** (no dark gray on black)

---

## TYPOGRAPHY SYSTEM

### Primary Font Families (Canva Free)

**For Headlines & Hooks:**
- **Bebas Neue** (bold, impactful, all caps)
- **Anton** (strong, modern)
- **Oswald** (elegant, sophisticated)

**For Body Text:**
- **Montserrat** (clean, professional)
- **Poppins** (modern, readable)
- **Raleway** (elegant, spacious)

### Font Pairing Rules:
- **Headline:** Bebas Neue or Anton
- **Body:** Montserrat or Poppins
- **Use only 2 fonts per carousel** (1 headline + 1 body)
- **All caps for hooks** (SLIDE 1 ONLY)

### Font Sizes (Instagram/LinkedIn 1080x1080):

**Slide 1 (Hook):**
- Main text: 90-120pt (Bebas Neue/Anton, all caps)
- Subtext (if needed): 40pt (Montserrat)

**Slides 2-8 (Content):**
- Main point: 50-70pt (Montserrat Bold)
- Supporting text: 30-40pt (Montserrat Regular)
- Slide numbers: 28pt (Montserrat Light)

**Slide 9 (Summary):**
- Header: 60pt (Bebas Neue)
- Bullet points: 35-45pt (Montserrat)

**Slide 10 (CTA):**
- Main CTA: 70-90pt (Bebas Neue)
- Handle: 40pt (Montserrat)

---

## LOGO USAGE

### Your Logo Files:
- Primary: White "DS MARKETING" on black background
- Use the logo as-is (don't modify the spacing or font)

### Logo Placement:

**Option A - Minimalist (Recommended):**
- Bottom right corner of slide 1 and slide 10
- Size: 15% of slide width
- 5% margin from edges

**Option B - Bold:**
- Center of slide 10 only
- Size: 40% of slide width
- Let it be the star

**Never:**
- Place logo on every slide (too cluttered)
- Make logo smaller than 10% width (too subtle)
- Place logo on busy backgrounds

---

## DESIGN PHILOSOPHY

### The DS Marketing Look:

**1. Minimalism First**
- Less is more
- Generous white space (30-40% of each slide)
- One main element per slide
- Clean, uncluttered layouts

**2. Bold Typography**
- Text is the hero
- Large, confident sizing
- High contrast for readability
- Strategic use of all caps

**3. Editorial Style**
- Magazine-quality layouts
- Sophisticated spacing
- Grid-based alignment
- Premium feel

**4. Negative Space Power**
- Don't fill every pixel
- Let content breathe
- Use emptiness strategically
- Balance is key

### Template 1: BOLD STATEMENT (Hook Slide)

```

|                                   |
|     YOUR BOLD HOOK                |
|     TEXT HERE IN                  |
|     ALL CAPS                      |

```

**Design Specs:**
- Background: Pure black (#000000)

- Logo: Bottom right, small, white


**Design Specs:**
- Background: Pure white (#FFFFFF)

- Supporting text: Dark gray (#1A1A1A), 35pt, Montserrat Regular
- Slide number: Bottom right, 28pt, light gray
- Alignment: Left-aligned, 10% margin from left edge

## DESIGN ELEMENTS TO USE

### Geometric Shapes (Minimal Use):

**Lines:**
- Thin horizontal dividers (2-4px)
- Use to separate sections
- Always black on white or white on black

**Rectangles:**
- Solid color blocks for emphasis
- Can be used as text backgrounds
- Always black or white, no fills

**Circles:**
- Around numbers (01, 02, etc.)
- Outline only (stroke, no fill)
- 4-6px stroke width

### What NOT to Use:
- Icons or illustrations (keep it type-focused)
- Stock photos (unless absolutely necessary - then high contrast B&W only)
- Patterns or textures
- Drop shadows or glows
- Decorative elements
- Emojis (text only)

---

## SPACING & ALIGNMENT

### The Grid System:

**Margins:**
- Left/Right: 10% of slide width
- Top/Bottom: 8% of slide height
- Example: On 1080px slide = 108px margin

**Element Spacing:**
- Between headline and body: 40-60px
- Between body paragraphs: 30px
- Line height: 130-150% of font size

**Alignment:**
- Content slides: Left-aligned
- Hook slide: Centered
- CTA slide: Centered
- Be consistent throughout carousel

## BRAND DON'TS (Never Do These)

- **Never use colors** - not even "just a little accent color"
- **Never use busy backgrounds** - solid colors only
- **Never use multiple fonts** - stick to 2 max
- **Never use small text** - minimum 35pt for body
- **Never overcrowd slides** - one point per slide
- **Never use low contrast** - always pure black on white
- **Never use decorative fonts** - clean and bold only
- **Never ignore white space** - it's your superpower
- **Never use stock imagery** - type is enough
- **Never forget slide numbers** - helps engagement

## DS MARKETING CAROUSEL CHECKLIST

Before you post ANY carousel:

**Design Check:**
- [ ] Pure black and white only (no colors)
- [ ] High contrast on every slide
- [ ] Logo appears on slide 1 or 10 (or both)
- [ ] Font sizes: 60pt+ for headlines, 40pt+ for body
- [ ] 30-40% white space on each slide
- [ ] Consistent alignment throughout
- [ ] All slides numbered (2/10, 3/10...)

**Content Check:**
- [ ] Hook slide grabs attention
- [ ] One point per content slide
- [ ] Summary slide included
- [ ] CTA is clear and actionable
- [ ] @dsmarketing.agency visible on slide 10
- [ ] No typos (read backwards to catch them)

**Brand Voice Check:**
- [ ] Confident and premium tone
- [ ] No fluff - direct and valuable
- [ ] Professional but not corporate
- [ ] Educational yet sophisticated
