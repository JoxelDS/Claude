## HOW TO POST (Step by Step)

### MONDAY (Instagram, 11 AM)
1. Go to business.facebook.com -> Create Post -> Instagram
2. Upload all 10 images from `week1/images/monday/` (in order)
3. Open `week1/captions/week1_monday_social_mistakes.md`
4. Copy the caption and paste it
5. Schedule for Monday 11:00 AM

### TUESDAY (LinkedIn, 10 AM)
1. Upload same Monday images to LinkedIn
2. Open `week1/captions/week1_linkedin_tuesday_thursday.md`
3. Copy the TUESDAY caption
4. Schedule for Tuesday 10:00 AM

### WEDNESDAY (Instagram, 11 AM)

2. Open `week1/captions/week1_wednesday_content_calendar.md`

4. Schedule for Wednesday 11:00 AM

### THURSDAY (LinkedIn, 10 AM)

3. Copy the THURSDAY caption
4. Schedule for Thursday 10:00 AM

### FRIDAY (Instagram, 11 AM)

4. Track responses in a spreadsheet
