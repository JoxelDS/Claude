## DS MARKETING BRAND VOICE GUIDELINES

**Tone:**
- Confident, not arrogant
- Direct, not aggressive
- Premium, not pretentious
- Knowledgeable, not condescending

**Language:**
- Use "you" and "your" (direct address)
- Short sentences for impact
- Active voice (not passive)
- Specific numbers and data

**Avoid:**
- Fluff and filler words
- Excessive exclamation marks
- Corporate jargon
- Overused buzzwords

**Sound Like:**
- A trusted advisor, not a salesperson
- An expert, not a guru
- A strategist, not an influencer
